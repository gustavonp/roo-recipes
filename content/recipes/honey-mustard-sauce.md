> Ideal for a quick salad dressing or a nice drizzle of a tangy sauce on your meals.



### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
- 1/2 cup honey
- 4 teaspoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons sugar

### Directions

1. Whisk all ingredients in a bowl until throughly blended.
